How to earn tokens?

The game has a built-in supply & demand mechanism that rewards farmers who are early to the game and drive scarcity. With no ICO or premine the only way to earn tokens is to farm crops or purchase on an exchange.

Farming tokens

You can earn tokens by planting crops, waiting paitently and harvesting rewards. Will you become a potato hustler or a parsnip pimp?

Halvening events

Like Bitcoin halvenings but more fun. As supply increases, rewards lessen. Those who hustle early are rewarded greatly for their efforts. Read more about the tokenomics here

Open source & extensible

The game is open source and built by the players. See the code for yourself.

Crowd-sourced Crafting

Craft ERC20 tools to gather resources and other ERC20 materials. Are you a collector? You can also craft a range of NFT decorations and collectables using resources you have gathered.

Recipes

Use Sunflower Tokens and other in-game resources to craft items, equipment and unique NFTs. View the crafting guide

Endless items

The game has a unique design which allows players to launch their own crafting recipes. Think minecraft but crowd sourced! See the code for yourself.

Ownership

You have complete ownership over your items. You can send them to friends, trade on exchanges or sell on NFT marketplaces like OpenSea.

Rewards for Hodlers

Earn distributed rewards every 3 days just by holding the token and supporting the game.

Collect your rewards.

Resources

Using your minted tools you can gather a range of resources to help you build your farm and craft new recipes. Chop, Mine, Fish, Fight, Build and more!

Crowd Sourced

How do I connect and play this game?
The game requires a Metamask account that is setup with the Polygon network. You can read more about connecting here
How do the developers make money?
The developers make tokens the same way as everyone else, by farming! In the future the plan will be to mint special edition NFTs and run resource gathering events. The team plans to fund the Zainist (ZAIN) project during these stages.
Can I close the browser?
As long as you save the game, you can close the browser. Take a rest and come back tomorrow to harvest your plants!
